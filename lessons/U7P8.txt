Li Xiang is a smart shopper. (李翔是个精明的购物者。)
He often shops in the supermarket. (他常去超市购物。)
Things there are not expensive, and he can make different choices. (那儿的商品不贵，他能做出不同选择。)
He always makes a shopping list and only buys things on the list. (他总会列购物清单，只买清单上的物品。)
This can help him save time and money as well. (这也有助于他节省时间和金钱。)
He also likes to look for sales, so he can buy things at good prices. (他也爱找促销，这样能买到实惠商品。)
Li Xiang is very careful when he is doing shopping. (李翔购物时十分细心。)
He always checks the price and date before he makes a choice. (做选择前，他总会检查价格和日期。)
And when he is making payments, he checks everything again. (付款时，他会再次检查所有物品。)
He is also very careful not to waste money. (他也很注意不浪费钱。)
For example, if he can share something with his friends, he never wastes money on the same thing. (比如，若能和朋友共用某样东西，他就不会在同样物品上浪费钱。)
