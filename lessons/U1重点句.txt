What's your dream job? (你梦想的工作是什么？)
I want to be an artist. (我想成为一名艺术家。)
How are you doing today? (你今天过得怎么样？)
What happened to your dog, Lingling? (玲玲，你的狗怎么了？)
But, thanks to the vets, it's getting well now. (但是，多亏了兽医，它现在正在好转。)
I'd like to be a vet like them. (我想成为像他们一样的兽医。)
I think I'm lively and I love dancing very much. (我认为我很活泼，而且我非常喜欢跳舞。)
They try to protect the environment and make the world a better place. (他们努力保护环境，让世界变得更美好。)
His story teaches us that our dreams will one day come true as long as we keep going. (他的故事告诉我们，只要我们坚持不懈，我们的梦想终有一天会实现。)
We will have a class meeting tomorrow. (我们明天将开一次班会。)
We are going to talk about dream jobs. (我们打算谈论梦想的工作。)
Will you share your opinion? (你会分享你的观点吗？)
The rain won't last long. (雨不会下很久。)
I know it is not easy to become a plane engineer and I will meet a lot of difficulties. (我知道成为一名飞机工程师不容易，而且我会遇到很多困难。)
However, I will try my best to make my dream come true. (然而，我会尽我最大的努力让我的梦想成真。)
